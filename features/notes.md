Drop a note anywhere on the chart to mark something worth remembering — a
good anchorage, a hazard, a spot to check next time you're through.

**To add a note**, open the edit menu (pencil icon) and choose **Add Note**,
then tap the chart where you want it.

**To work with an existing note**, tap it on the chart. By default this
opens a quick popover with **Move**, **Edit**, **Delete**, and **Info**
(the info panel or a details dialog, depending on your **Prefer Info-Panel**
setting under **Settings → Display**). If you'd rather skip that popover and
go straight to the note's details, turn on **Single Click for Note Details**
in the same settings section — a single tap then opens the details directly,
and the details view itself offers **Move**, **Edit**, and **Delete**, so
you don't lose anything by skipping the popover.

A note with rich content — an embedded image, or a graph like the one on a
tide-station note — resizes its details dialog to fit, instead of clipping
the content or forcing you to scroll in two directions.
