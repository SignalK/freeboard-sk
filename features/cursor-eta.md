![Fig 1. The Live ETA at cursor setting, and the resulting Brg/Dist/ETA readout in the status bar](cursor-eta-1.jpg)

Get a quick bearing, distance, and time estimate to any point on the chart,
just by pointing at it — without setting a destination first.

**To turn it on**, go to **Settings → Display → Status Bar Options** and
check **Live ETA at cursor**. As you move the mouse over the chart, the
status bar adds the bearing, distance, and ETA from your boat to the cursor,
alongside the existing latitude/longitude readout. It's off by default and
only updates as the mouse moves, so it's a planning aid for anyone at a
desktop or laptop rather than something you'll see on a touchscreen at the
helm.

The ETA uses your boat's current speed over ground. If you're stopped — at
anchor, hove to — there's no speed to estimate from, so Freeboard falls back
to a **Reference Boat Speed** you set alongside the checkbox, keeping the
ETA useful even when you're not moving.
